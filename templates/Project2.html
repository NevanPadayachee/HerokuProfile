Students Math Results

The data was obtained in a survey of students math courses in secondary school. The data was collected from two schools, Gabriel Pereira and Mousinho da Silverira It contains a lot of interesting social, gender and study information about students. The following diagram shows the correlation between many of the important datasets.

There is Positive correlation between parents education and final result as well as a Positively strong correlation between the first two tests and the final test.

There is negative correlation between age and the final result,a negative correlation between free time and the final test, a negative correlation between travel time and final test and a negative correlation between going out There is a negatively strong correlation between failures and final result.

The following is a diagram showing the negative correlation between the failures and the final test result.

The following is a diagram showing the positive correlation between the mothers education and the final test result.

The conclusion

Students whose parents have studied past secondary school have a slightly higher chance of doing well in math. Students whose travel time is high, have more free time and have failed classes multiple times have a lower chance of doing well in math.

The datasets were obtained from kaggle where you can get many free datasets and can enter many kaggle competitions orientated around data science and data analytics. The machine learning model was created with the help of the tensorflow website.
